Lucas-Adopted!

Neutered Male

2 Years

12 Pounds

Spaniel Mix

Meet the adorable Lucas.
Lucas is an all around happy guy who loves other dogs of all sizes but prefers to get down and play with dogs his size. He would enjoy a life where he is part of the family and included in the weekend plans. He is friendly with people, including children, he has a wag for everyone he meets and he loves a good cuddle. He has a thick soft coat that will need brushing once in a while and cute little folded over ears.
Lucas may do best with another dog in his new home so when you are away he has some company (although it is not a must). He has been going to doggie daycare everyday and he LOVES it! He is just a social little guy with lots of love to give.
Lucas is 2 years old, neutered, potty trained and pee pad trained (he is working on crate training in his foster home and doing very well). Lucas rides well in the car and he has some basic training like sit and down.
All in all Lucas is a wonderful little guy waiting for a loving home, could that be yours?
