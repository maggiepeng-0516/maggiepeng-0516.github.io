Remy-Adopted!

Neutered Male

9 Months

60 Pounds

Husky Mix

Do you love the outdoors? Is hiking your thing? Do you love to spend the day exploring the beach?
Then we’ve got the perfect dog for you!
Meet Remy! He is a gorgeous goofy energetic puppy, he loves attention and is very smart. He is sure to win you over with his good looks and bouncy personality.
Remy loves other dogs but can be a bit much for some, especially the little guys, he is still learning a proper introduction. He learns quickly though and is food motivated, he will be a shining star of his puppy class for sure (training will be strongly suggested for this sweet guy). He knows some of the basics already, Sit, down, paw, touch and he’s working on re-call with his foster.
The perfect family for Remy will be active, loving, patient, and because of Remy’s breed and size, provide boundaries until he learns some manners. His new people/person should have a commitment to continued training and strong leadership.
Remy is great with people. He loves ‘em! We are not sure about small children as he hasn’t really met many in his 9 months. We are guessing older children 12 and above will do fine with this ball of silly energy. Remy has never lived with cats so we just don’t know how he will do with them.
Remy is UTD on vaccines, neutered, fully potty trained, and working on crate and leash training. He is working on being alone--his mild separation anxiety is getting better and better every day.
Remy is a very healthy happy puppy looking for his active forever family.
Could that be you?
