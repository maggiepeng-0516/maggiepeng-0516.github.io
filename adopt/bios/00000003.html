Bodhi-Adopted!

Neutered Male

3 Years

Unknown Breed

Bodhi is a 3yr old male mystery breed from an Asian meat market. He was left at a rescue, that has since closed down, where Good Samaritans pulled him. He's been fostered since March in San Leandro by a very dedicated foster family with two dogs about Bodhi's size. Bodhi enjoys following his foster siblings around, much to the chagrin of his sis, and especially loves doing whatever his brother Dax is doing.
Bodhi is up to date on vaccinations, and neutered. He is crate trained and dog friendly with dogs 40 lb+. No small dogs for this guy (we assume cats are out, too). Bodhi would be fine in a family with older children; he loves and bonds tightly to his people. Though sometimes a timid guy at first, he becomes a big love bug. He enjoys hiking, leisurely walks, watching window "tv," and snoozing on couches. Bodhi has a little separation anxiety, really understandable considering his humble beginnings. He has been working with trainers, and his foster will pass on his expensive aluminum crate to his new family.
We think Bodhi will make some lucky person/family very happy
