Vineyard Litter

Pitbull

The Vineyard Litter came to us with the help of one of our favorite partner shelters, San Francisco Animal Care and Control. Momma, a super sweet Pit Bull lady was found by a good samaritan who, when he realized she was pregnant, couldn’t bear to let her stay on the streets. Three days after the puppies were born he realized he wasn’t equipped to care for the whole family and did the right thing: he asked for help. Momma and the pups were very fortunate to stay in the home of an SFACC helper for their first 5 weeks before coming to us. All 9 puppies have had plenty of time with their mother, great socialization with their litter-mates, and are now learning about people and the outside world in their foster environment.
ALL APPLICANTS MUST PROVIDE PROOF OF RESIDENCE OR LANDLORD/HOA PERMISSION TO BE ELIGIBLE FOR ADOPTION. WE ENCOURAGE YOU TO RESEARCH ANY POWERFUL BREED DOG TO DETERMINE IF THEY ARE A FIT FOR YOUR HOME.
